About EPX Labs

EPX Labs is a full-service, end-to-end IT Managed Services Partner (MSP). Our laboratory was founded based on the principle of challenging each and every costly IT assumption of the past. We believe in implementing Faster, Simpler, Cheaper, and Reliable IT solutions.

EPX Labs achieves success through:

Technology Agnosticism

We choose the best tools to solve each of your specific problems. This often means Clojure.

Cost-Consciousness

We build strong, long-term client-partnerships focused on delivering savings and recurring value.

Embracing DevOps (APIOps, NoOps, etc.) as a Philosophy

We make the customer’s experience more enjoyable and your product development life-cycle better, faster, and stronger.

Utilizing Serverless Micro/Nano-Service Architectures Everywhere

We architect IT systems which require less hands-on management thanks to automation and ris distribution. Our designs, by default, enable antifragility, high-availability, and scalability.

Passion

We’re passionate about what we do -- from clean commit histories to elegant abstraction through thoughtful analysis. You found us because you are passionate about what you do, as well. Together, we will build great tools.

Mission

With his partner Evan Sinicin, Prachetas Prabhu founded EPX Labs, a DevOps consultancy committed to offering consultant services while developing cutting edge technology. Prachetas qualifies EPX Labs as an “outgrowth of himself.”

“It’s not a cut & dry consultancy,” he says. “It is an innovation company with a consulting arm, a place to experiment with prototypes from which new products like NoOps, or serverless operations, can be developed. Thus the laboratory.”

And as added value, in this day and age of bottom-line-only thinking, Prachetas is also committed to making affordable IT services available to the community as a way to “give back.”
